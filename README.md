# ትግሜድ — ናይ ትግርኛ ሕክምና ሓበሬታ

**ትግሜድ** is a Tigrinya-language health information assistant built for communities in Tigray, Ethiopia and the broader Tigrinya-speaking diaspora.

Health information in your own language is a right, not a privilege. This project exists to close the gap.

---

## What it does

- Answers health questions in Tigrinya
- Covers nutrition, hygiene, and mental health
- Works on any phone, including low-bandwidth connections
- Built with and reviewed by health professionals from Tigray

## Status

Early stage. The knowledge base is small and growing. Every answer has been written with clinical care and always directs users to consult a health worker for anything serious.

## Contributing

We welcome contributions from:

- **Health professionals** — doctors, nurses, and health extension workers from Tigray or the diaspora who can review and improve answers
- **Tigrinya speakers** — anyone who can help write, translate, or evaluate content for naturalness and cultural appropriateness
- **Developers** — the stack is Next.js, contributions welcome

If you want to contribute, open an issue or reach out directly.

## Disclaimer

ትግሜድ provides health information only — it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. In any emergency, go to a health facility immediately.
